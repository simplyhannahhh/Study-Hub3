Biology: The Cell

All living organisms are composed of cells. Cells are the basic unit of life and are the smallest structural and functional unit of an organism, typically microscopic and consisting of cytoplasm and a nucleus enclosed in a membrane. They are the building blocks of all life forms, from bacteria to complex multicellular organisms like humans.
